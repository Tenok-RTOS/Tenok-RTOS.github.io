    • UART1 (console): PA9 (TX), PB7 (RX)

    • Select by enabling include platform/stm32f429disc.mk in the Makefile
    • UART1 (console): PA9 (TX), PB7 (RX)
    • UART3 (debug-link): PC10 (TX), PC11 (RX)
